Temperature Tuning

Print temperature affects adhesion, strength, and stringing.

Cooling

Filament Materials

PLA

Easy to print, biodegradable, perfect for beginners and decorative parts.

PETG

Stronger and more heat resistant than PLA with minimal warping.

ABS

Durable and heat resistant, but requires enclosure and ventilation.

TPU

Flexible filament ideal for phone cases, seals, and gaskets.

PC

Strong and durable filament ideal for functional parts.

Impact PLA

Impact-resistant filament ideal for functional parts.

Common Print Problems

Warping

Use bed adhesion aids, correct bed temperature, and avoid drafts.

Stringing

Lower nozzle temperature and enable retraction in slicer settings.

Layer Shifts

Check belt tension and ensure motors are not overheating.

Safety & Maintenance

Ventilation

Always ventilate when printing ABS, nylon, or carbon fiber materials.

Maintenance

Regularly clean nozzles, lubricate rails, and level the bed.

Fire Safety

Never leave printers unattended for long prints without safeguards.
